Sylvan Lake Picnic


INSDA is planning a trip to Sylvan Lake on the 19th of July. We will be providing breakfast, lunch and transportation to and fro. This is a wonderful opportunity to catch up with the Indian Student Community in Edmonton. But this trip is not restricted to just students of U of A. Anyone is welcome!

Independence Day


The great nation of India became independent from the Kingdom of Great Britain on August 15, 1947. Since then, the day is filled with pride and joy for all Indians, worldwide. Come and join the Indian Students' Association in celebrating India's independence with a colourful evening of music, dance, and displays of patriotism on the eve of Independence Day at the Students' Union Building's SUB Stage, at the University of Alberta. This is a complimentary event, sponsored by INDSA. Everyone is welcome to attend. Snacks and refreshments will be served. For any questions, concerns, and/or comments, please email us at indsa@ualbera.ca, or Facebook message us. We hope to see your smiling faces at the event.
