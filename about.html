Welcome to INDSA!

INDSA has always been like a family to the Indian students who are away from home and has helped them right from the point they arrive at the airport till they get settled in their new academic and social setting at the university. Our members have reciprocated by being a huge support in terms of participation and volunteering for INDSA events and activities.

Our Purpose

To create and extend support for cultural awareness, provide a platform for participation in campus activities, establish contact with the Indian community, and promote international understanding and fellowship.

Our Composition

INDSA currently represents about 200 graduate and undergraduate students, faculty and alumni at the University of Alberta and is fast expanding with each coming year. Each year new students are provided with relocation support and basic guidance in their settlement through designated volunteers.

Our Structure

The association constitutes of eight elected volunteer executives, who represent the member body at large, and it is primarily supported as a collective effort of all the members within the association.
