Namaste!!

INDSA, the Indian Students' Association at the University of Alberta, is a non-profit socio-cultural association within the student organizations at the University of Alberta, Edmonton. Started in 1991 as a small initiative, today it has grown to represent hundreds of students and alumni who form the nucleus of the Indian community in Edmonton and beyond.

The foremost design of the association is to preserve, nurture and extend the cultural traditions and colors of India within the University. INDSA celebrates the diversity of the Indian culture and invites members from across all nationalities to share the Indian experience with the association.

A family away from home, a place to share and celebrate, a platform to show your talents - the association is an effort to draw from each and every member the very best of the Indian culture and to share with the community at large , the myriad of colors and the rich heritage that embody India.
